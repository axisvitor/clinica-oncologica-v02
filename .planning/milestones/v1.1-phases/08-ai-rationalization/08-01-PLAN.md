<objective>
Remove 5 single-node LangGraph StateGraph wrappers (humanization, sentiment, generation, question_variation, empathetic_follow_up) and migrate ALL callers to direct GeminiClient.generate_content() calls.

Purpose: Eliminate unnecessary LangGraph orchestration overhead (state serialization, checkpoint I/O, graph compilation) for what are effectively single async function calls. The multi-node graphs (flow_message, flow_response, consensus) with real routing logic are preserved.

Output: All 5 single-node graphs removed from graphs.py, all callers migrated, helper functions preserved in nodes_ai.py, tests updated.
</objective>
