- Change `def _commit_flow_state_with_lock(self, flow_state, action_name)` to `async def _commit_flow_state_with_lock(self, flow_state, action_name)`

- Update ALL call sites of `_commit_flow_state_with_lock` to use `await self._commit_flow_state_with_lock(...)` (research says lines ~375, ~468, ~530)
