<objective>
Implement WhatsApp opt-out handler that detects STOP/PARAR/CANCELAR keywords, revokes consent, and immediately halts messaging for the patient.

Purpose: LGPD Art. 18 requires immediate and unconditional response to consent revocation. Currently, there is no mechanism to stop messaging when a patient sends an opt-out keyword via WhatsApp.

Output: Patient model with messaging_stopped_at column, opt-out interception in webhook message handler, and outbound send guard in UnifiedWhatsAppService.
</objective>
