<objective>
Wire failed flow messages into the DLQ with retry policy and monitoring integration, so that messages that fail delivery after all Celery retries are captured, visible in the dashboard, and retried automatically.

Purpose: Currently, flow messages that fail after max Celery retries are marked as FAILED in the database but silently lost. They never reach the DLQ monitoring dashboard and are never retried. This creates a data integrity gap where patients may miss critical treatment messages without operational visibility.

Output: Modified `send_scheduled_message` task that routes to DLQ on final failure; DLQ retry config using exponential backoff (30s, 2min, 10min per user decision); unit tests.
</objective>
