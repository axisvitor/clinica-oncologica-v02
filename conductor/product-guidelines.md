# Product Guidelines

## Communication & Voice
- **Language:** Portuguese (BR).
- **Tone:** Direct and Efficient. Communication should be concise, clinically accurate, and focused on clarity and speed. Avoid unnecessary fluff or overly complex prose.

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Style:** Minimalist and Functional.
- **Design Philosophy:** Prioritize content and data over decorative elements. Use a "content-first" approach that favors functional clarity.
- **Data Density:** The interface, especially the clinical dashboard, should favor high information density to allow medical staff to see maximum relevant data with minimal navigation.

## User Experience (UX)
- **Clinical Dashboard:**
    - Focus on **Information Density**: Display comprehensive clinical data efficiently.
    - Optimized for desktop and tablet usage.
- **Patient Quiz Interface:**
    - **Single-Question View:** Present one question at a time to ensure patient focus and reduce cognitive load during health reporting.
    - Optimized for mobile-first interaction (WhatsApp-to-Web flow).

## Accessibility & Usability
- **Standards:** Strict adherence to **WCAG 2.1** compliance.
- **Contrast:** Ensure high contrast for legibility.
- **Interactions:** Predictable and consistent navigation patterns across all modules.
